French-Style Meat (МЯСО ПО-ФРАНЦУЗСКИ)

Description

French-Style Meat comprises pork steaks layered with potatoes and onions, sprinkled with cheese and topped with (originally) sour cream, or more recently, mayonnaise.

Ingredients

  • Peeled potatoes
  • Thinly sliced veal
  • Garlic mayonnaise
  • Mayonnaise
  • Onions
  • Mushrooms
  • salt and pepper
  • cheese
  • oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F/200°C. Lightly oil a rectangular baking pan.
  2. Cut the potatoes in half, thinly slicing to maximum ¼ inch thick pieces. Layer the potatoes evenly in the baking pan. Add salt and pepper.
  3. Cut the onions into quarters and thinly slice. Separate the onion pieces from each other. Layer half the onions over the potatoes. Generously drizzle with Garlic Aioli Mayo.
  4. Layer the thin veal pieces across the pan. Add salt and pepper.
  5. Add the mushrooms and onions over the meat. Drizzle with mayonnaise and spread until covered evenly.
  6. Bake in the oven for about 45 minutes. Remove and sprinkle with grated cheese and bake again for 10 minutes until the cheese is melted and golden.
